    Our Story

    Haris Architectural Systems was established to bridge the gap between exceptional manufacturing capabilities and the growing demand for superior aluminium and glass products in the UK. With a foundation rooted in innovation and excellence, we have quickly become a trusted name in the industry, known for our commitment to quality and customer satisfaction.

    What Makes us unique

  • In-House Production

    By extruding aluminium in-house, we eliminate the need for third-party processing, reducing lead times and costs

  • Bespoke Solutions

    Every product we create is tailored to the specific needs of our clients, ensuring a perfect fit for every project

  • Sustainability

    Our streamlined processes mean less waste and quicker turnaround times, with all unused aluminium recycled for further use.

  • High Quality
