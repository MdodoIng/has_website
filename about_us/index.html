    About HAS

    Haris Architectural Systems has over 25 years’ experience in the aluminium and glass industry. We are the solution to all your needs. In a market where you have to deliver results, HAS invested €20M into extrusion solutions and now control the entire chain allowing us to design, extrude, manufacture and install our own aluminium products.

    Our capabilities are exceptional in glass manufacture, with millions of euros invested to manufacture our own glass and have our own powder coating facilities. Our state-of-the-art manufacturing facility, latest technology and quality control systems mean we only produce the best quality products, at affordable prices.

    With a team of over 125 skilled professionals, we’re dedicated to saving you time and money by streamlining processes and reducing lead times. Whether you need aluminium products or steel structures, we’ve got you covered. Our commitment to quality and excellence makes us the ideal partner to help you achieve success in today’s fast-paced market.
