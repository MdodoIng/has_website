    Services we ensure

  • Design with you in collaboration

    At Haris Architectural Systems, we believe that the best results come from a collaborative approach. That’s why we work closely with you throughout the entire design process, ensuring that your vision is fully realized in every aspect of the project. From initial concept to final execution, our team of experts partners with you to understand your specific needs, preferences, and goals.

  • Extrude the aluminium in house

    At Haris Architectural Systems, we take pride in our ability to extrude aluminium in-house, giving us complete control over the entire production process. By managing this critical step internally, we ensure that every aluminium component meets our exacting standards for quality, durability, and performance.n-house extrusion also offers unparalleled flexibility, enabling us to create bespoke profiles tailored to your specific project requirements. Whether you need custom shapes, unique designs, or precise dimensions, our advanced extrusion technology can deliver with exceptional accuracy.

  • Manufacturing

    Our advanced manufacturing facility is equipped with the latest technology for aluminium and glass production. With a focus on innovation and sustainability, we produce high-strength, corrosion-resistant aluminium and durable, aesthetically pleasing glass. Our rigorous quality control processes ensure that every product we manufacture is built to last, delivering exceptional performance across a wide range of applications.

  • Delivery

    Our efficient supply chain and logistical expertise ensure timely delivery of all products, regardless of project scale. By managing the entire process in-house, we can optimize lead times and guarantee that your order arrives exactly when you need it, reducing downtime and keeping your project on schedule.

  • Installation

    With over 25 years of experience, our skilled installation teams provide seamless integration of our products into your development. Whether it’s aluminium curtain walling, windows, doors, or glass structures, our professionals ensure that each installation is completed with precision and care. Our commitment to excellence means that your project not only meets but exceeds expectations, delivering results that stand the test of time.
